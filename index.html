Terms of Service

1. Acceptance of Terms

By invoking the Jeeves Discord bot (via the /ask slash command, direct messages, or other interaction models), you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service. If you do not agree, please remove the bot from your server or cease interactions with it.

2. Description of Service

Jeeves is an artificial intelligence-powered assistant that leverages third-party large language models to provide contextual answers, chat continuity, and long-term memory features across Discord servers and direct messages.

3. Availability and Disclaimers

The service is provided on an "as-is" and "as-available" basis. The bot's operator makes no guarantees regarding continuous uptime, API availability from external vendors, or the flawless performance of specific commands (such as data clearing utilities). Underlying storage layers may experience temporary infrastructure outages, during which certain bot features may degrade or fail gracefully.

Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect and Process

To provide chat responses and remember context, Jeeves processes and stores the following specific data points:

Data Type Storage Duration Operational Purpose
Discord User ID Indefinite (Until deleted) The primary unique numeric identifier used to route messages, apply rate limits, and safely isolate long-term memories.
Discord Channel ID Temporary (Up to 48 hours) Cached temporarily inside automated session keys to isolate and maintain short-term chat history threads within specific public channels.
Message & Reply Text Temporary (Up to 48 hours) Short-term conversation history is truncated to a maximum of 500 characters per turn and automatically expires after 48 hours of thread inactivity.
Extracted Facts & Vectors Indefinite (Until deleted) Synthesized third-person facts extracted from your text by AI, alongside mathematical vector embeddings, to provide long-term personalized context. Raw prompts are not stored here.
Crucial Disclosure regarding Cross-Context Memory: Long-term facts extracted by Jeeves follow your specific Discord User ID across different channels, servers, and direct message scopes. Insights or context learned during a conversation in one server or a private DM window may inform and contextualize the replies Jeeves generates for you in an entirely different server environment.

2. Third-Party Data Processors

Jeeves passes conversation text, cached history, and extracted facts to Google Gemini API endpoints to generate chat completions and produce mathematical vector embeddings. Data shared with third-party APIs is governed by their respective developer policies. Jeeves does not collect or store your Discord username, display name, email, IP address, or payment details.

3. User Controls and Deletion Rights

You maintain control over your data via the native /forget-me confirm:True slash command. Executing this command triggers a routine that wipes your temporary chat history caches, clears your rate-limiting state, and purges all long-term vector facts associated with your Discord User ID.

Infrastructure Outage Disclaimer: Data deletion routines rely on the live availability of our underlying database containers. In the event of an infrastructure error or database timeout, the deletion pipeline may fail to execute fully to preserve system stability, leaving short-term cached data or rate limits temporarily intact. If you encounter an error message while executing a wipe, please contact the system operator to process your request manually.

4. Contact Information

For manual data privacy inquiries, deletion escalation requests, or technical support, please contact the bot operator at the designated technical contact point provided in the application's Discord directory profile.
